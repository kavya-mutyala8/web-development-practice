Our Signature Brews

🥛

Swathi iced Latte

Espresso-Based / Cold Milk-Based Beverage

A refreshing and smooth classic made with rich, freshly pulled espresso shots poured over chilled milk and a generous serving of ice cubes. It delivers a perfect balance of bold coffee flavor and creamy, crisp milk—ideal for a warm day.

☁️

Swathi Macha Latte

Tea-Based Specialty Latte (Caffeine Alternative)

A vibrant and earthy alternative to coffee, made from high-quality stone-ground green tea leaves (matcha) whisked smooth and combined with perfectly steamed, velvety milk. Rich in antioxidants and subtly sweet, it delivers a smooth, clean boost of energy without the coffee jitters.

🍫

Swathi Masala Coffee

Spiced / Specialty Milk-Based Beverage

A warm, aromatic blend of bold espresso or traditional drip coffee combined with creamy milk and an infusion of fragrant Indian spices—including cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and ginger. It delivers a deeply comforting, subtly sweet, and beautifully spiced twist on your daily brew.

🎯

Swathi Filter Coffee

The Marked Espresso

A beloved South Indian classic made by brewing finely ground coffee powder and chicory in a traditional metal drip filter. The resulting thick, dark decoction is combined with boiling, frothy milk and sugar, then expertly poured back and forth between a tumbler and a davarah to create a signature velvety foam top.

🎯

Swathi Coffee

Standard Milk-Based Hot Coffee (Instant / Cafe Blend)

The comforting, familiar classic we all know and love. Made from a perfect blend of rich, smooth coffee and piping hot milk, lightly frothed and sweetened just right. It is a simple, balanced, and deeply satisfying brew that gives you that perfect, comforting everyday coffee taste.

🎯

Swathi Espresso

Pure Concentrated Espresso Shot (The Foundation of Coffee)

A bold, intense, and deeply concentrated shot of coffee forced through finely ground beans under high pressure. Full-bodied and aromatic, it features a signature rich, golden layer of "crema" on top that locks in the complex and robust flavor notes.

🎯

Swathi Black Coffee

Pure Brewed Coffee (Zero Milk)

A clean, unadorned classic made by brewing freshly ground coffee beans in hot water without any added milk, cream, or sugar. It highlights the bean's true origin, delivering a smooth, bold body with rich, naturally complex flavor notes and a crisp finish.

🎯

Swathi Thai Milk Tea

Sweet Tea-Based Specialty Beverage (Non-Coffee)

A strikingly vibrant, orange-hued classic brewed from strongly concentrated Ceylon tea leaves and infused with aromatic star anise, crushed tamarind seeds, and orange blossom. Served over crushed ice and layered with sweet condensed milk, it delivers a rich, creamy, and wonderfully sweet flavor profile.

🎯

Swathi Blueberry Latte

Flavored / Specialty Espresso Beverage

A sweet and whimsical twist on a classic latte, featuring freshly pulled espresso and velvety steamed milk infused with a vibrant, fruity blueberry syrup. It strikes a delightful balance between the bold richness of coffee and the sweet, tangy notes of ripe summer berries.
